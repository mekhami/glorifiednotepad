# Setup

After defeating Amelia and scouring the ship for weapons to use against the cosmic beast that is tearing the ship apart, the crew ready themselves to free themselves for good. Unbeknownst to them, a group of pirates below have rallied to retake the ship, and the captain of the ship, injured, waits in the cabin to ambush whoever enters. The entire situation teeters on the brink! 

At the end of our last session, most of the people on board the ship were shaken to the ground by the convulsions of the astral kraken. I will say that it takes the full round for everyone to regain their footing, which will advance the kraken timer and call for yet another random encounter check.

```
[E:Kraken Timer 7/10]
d: d6=1 Random Encounter!
```

Let's address this after we reintroduce the party!

As the group covers their ears in a futile attempt to drown out the nightmare sound of the kraken, two tentacles whip over the gunwale, furiously seeking out prey. Amchutchen and Leith are made quick work of, dust in the eyes of this vast and unknowable creature.

Their deaths shock the group, but Rawyn seizes the other bottle and loads it into the bombard.

```

```

Rawyn fires the bombard again. The bottle sails through the aether, trailing aether dust behind it. It careens off course, missing the kraken's body by inches, before exploding harmlessly in the abyss behind it.

The party stares in horror as the kraken's tentacles wrap around the fore of the ship in its entirety.

The survivors stare at each other silently. None acknowledge the insanity of the trauma they have just endured; none state the obvious - that they are marooned and none of them are equipped to pilot the remains of this vessel. Wordlessly, one by one, they separate, finding different places to search, to salvage what little they can from this situation.

A stunned silence settles over the room. Only loud breathing and the crackling of dying fires elsewhere on the ship. The survivors wipe blood from their hands and faces.

After surveying the room and seeing the controls of the ship, the rest of the remaining survivors, few as they are, sweep the rest of the vessel. No more stragglers remain, and there is no sign of the astral kraken.

The survivors will be left to pick up shattered pieces and try to make their way back to some form of civilization.
